ASUS ROG Strix G is introduced:
A gaming equipment worthy of your consideration!

There are friends who are paying attention to the gaming laptop. It should be no stranger to ASUS Rog Strix series! The Rog Strix is divided into many series according to different game types and specifications. However, if you are not specialized in a certain type of game, and want to have a more balanced hardware or software choice and a more user-friendly price, you can choose from the Rog Strix G series. There are 15 inch and 17 inch options for Rog Strix g. this introduction is Rog Strix g531gt.

The specifications of ASUS Rog Strix g (g531gt) are as follows:

  • Running Windows 10 system
  • 15.6 "FHD 120Hz screen
  • Intel Core i7-9750h processor
  • NVIDIA GTX 1660TI Graphic card
  • 8GB RAM
  • 512GB SSD
  • 1 USB type-C , 3 USB type-A , 1 HDMI 2.0, 1 3.5mm headphone and 1 RJ45 LAN jack port
  • 360(W) x 275(D) x 26 (H) mm
  • 2.395 kg
First of all, what I got here is the version of i7-9750H processor + GTX 1660Ti graphics card, as well as the configuration version with i5-9300h, GTX 1050, GTX 1650, etc. on the screen, the maximum refresh rate of 120Hz and 144hz can be seen. In addition to the 15.6-inch screen obtained by me, there is a larger version of 17.3-inch. Back to the computer, we can see that Strix G is taking the all black line. At the same time, we can see from the cover of the fuselage that the striation processing of the oblique section is very close to other Rog notebooks nowadays.

Design
The fuselage is mainly black, but when you look closely, you can find quite fine drawing texture, which is very clear under the reflection of certain optical fibers. Another thing I have to talk about is the eye mark of "the eye of the loser". However, it should be noted that this logo is not RGB light, but pure bright silver with metal decoration.
The heat dissipation design of Strix G is also a major feature. You can see that there is a protruding part at the rear of the fuselage. This protruding part provides more air flow space. During heat dissipation, air can be introduced through the upper part and the hot air can be discharged through the rear cooling hole. Under such a design, heat dissipation is naturally worth looking forward to, but it also sacrifices portability. This outstanding part widens the body of the Strix g with a 15.6-inch screen. Some backpacks that could hold 15.6-inch laptop may not be able to hold this computer. If it is not placed in the interlayer, it can be carried away, but it means that it has lost its protection.
Another feature of Strix G is the light belt at the bottom of the fuselage. Except for the rear, this light belt is directly extended by a light belt to cover the front, left and right. Because of the multi-layer design at the bottom of the fuselage, when using the computer, you will notice that the light under the pen will emit different colors, which looks very cool! The light band is adjusted with the keyboard back light. You can adjust it according to your preference, or you can choose to turn it off.

Perfomance

ROG Strix G is capable of handling multitasking and switching between serious gaming and production activity at ease, as it features the latest 9th Gen Intel Core i7-9750H processor and 8GB RAM. It also equips the NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1660Ti graphics, so you can expect stunning, immersive visuals on this machine.



Talking about its cooling systems, the 3D Flow Zone at the rear and Dual N-Blade fans increase airflow to ensure efficient cooling performance. The ultra-thin fins expand the surface area for faster heat dissipation too. You can switch between Silent, Balanced and Turbo modes to optimize performance for different games or applications.



Benchmarks

  • 3D Mark

    • 5,403 is a decent overall score for the 3D Mark benchmark test. Strix G gets 5,469 on the graphics score and 30-36 FPS in both graphics tests, while for the CPU test, it gets 17 FPS and a score of 5,061.



  • PC Mark 10

    • The machine gets 8,773 for App Start-up Score and 5,999 for Digital Creation Score. This shows that Strix G is a great laptop to handle both multitasking and digital creation score. Meanwhile, the overall score it gets is 5,150, which is a pretty good score compared to other gaming laptops.



  • UserBenchmark

    • UserBenchmark gives Strix G an 80% on gaming, showing that it can handle recent 3D games at high resolutions and ultra details levels. The SSD score is exceptional at 147%, the machine can provide outstanding performance on everyday tasks and workstation use.



Conclusion
In general, under the high-end gaming laptop with high configuration and various special functions, the overall configuration and functions of Strix G are relatively special members. In other words, the keystone function on Strix hreo III and the ultra-thin design of Zephyrus will not be seen on this computer. But it is undoubtedly the most user-friendly laptop in Rog series products, and on this basis, it also continues the cool appearance design of Rog and the mainstream function of RGB light belt. I think Strix G is a highly recommended computer.
